Compassion in Action

Building Bridges of Unity Through Compassionate Service

Nanak Ka Ghar is a secular nonprofit organization dedicated to fostering peace, unity, and community welfare through education, healthcare, and inclusive social development.

Our Guiding Mission

We work to transform silence into action, creating tangible change in communities where inequality persists.

Objectives & Vision

  • Break the silence on critical social issues through education and advocacy
  • Provide essential healthcare services to underserved communities
  • Create safe spaces that foster community support and dialogue
  • Promote inclusive development that bridges cultural and religious divides
